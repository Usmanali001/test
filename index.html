Artwork Set Up

Please set up your artwork to the following specifications:

  • Size with bleed: 1860 x 690mm
  • Resolution:: 150dpi minimum
  • Format: PDF or JPEG
  • Colour: CMYK (RGB/Pantones will be converted during proofing)
  • Fonts: Outlined or Embedded
  • Bleed: 3mm with no printer marks
  • Safe Zone: 3mm
  • Artwork Templates: PDF, PNG, JPEG
Key Information
  • VAT: 20% (included in price)
  • Delivery: Included in price
  • Turnaround: Artwork must be approved by 5:00pm for manual proofing and 6:30pm for online proofing to receive your order by the expected delivery date.
  • Same Day Jobs: Artwork must be approved by 5:00pm to receive your order on your expected delivery date.
  • Orders of 250 business cards or less will be delivered by Royal Mail.
